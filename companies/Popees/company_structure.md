# Company Structure - Popees Baby Care Products Limited (PBCPL)

PBCPL is a public limited company incorporated in India on 08 June 2016 (CIN U51900KL2016PTC045955), based in Manjeri / Malappuram, Kerala. It was a private limited company through FY25 and was converted to a public limited company shortly after the close of FY25 (per the Board's Report), consistent with its stated ambition to list on NSE / merge with a BSE entity. Shaju Thomas (Managing Director / CMD) holds 73.76% of equity; Linta P Jose (Director) holds 0.77%; the balance is held by other / institutional shareholders following a large FY25 securities-premium raise.

The wider "Popees Group" spans 12 related entities (job-workers, FG suppliers, retail/FOCO operators, an LLP running store operations, and several non-operational shells). Per the group-structure sheet, several are being merged into PBCPL over the next 3-12 months (Popees Fashions India Pvt Ltd, Ourkids Media, Seataal Properties, and the Popees Baby Care Products LLP store-operations vehicle before Dec 2026); others continue at arm's length (Esthana Designs - job work; Esther Apparels - FOCO stores run by a director's brother). Related-party trading with these entities (purchases, sales, job work) was material in FY24 and reduced sharply in FY25.

Analysis basis: standalone PBCPL financials only. Consolidated financials were not provided. Given the planned mergers and the active intra-group trading, consolidation scope is a diligence item. Flag: analysis based on standalone financials only; consolidated financials not provided.

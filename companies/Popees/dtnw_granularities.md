## E. Rule 5 - Debtors > 180 days as % of revenue

- Using the provided 31 Mar 2026 ageing report (matches the provisional period): debtors >180 days = **18.94 Cr** / FY26 revenue 162.00 Cr = **11.69%** -> exceeds the 10% ceiling. Rule 5 NOT met (borderline).
- FY25 audited basis gives the same read: 15.63 / 135.30 = 11.55%. The breach is consistent across both periods, not a one-off.

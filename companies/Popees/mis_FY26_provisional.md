This yields a stable gross margin of ~33-35% across FY24-FY26.

## Contribution-margin build (FY26, for Section 1 Rule 3 and Section 4)

- Revenue (operations, net) = 162.0031 Cr
- less COGS 107.8979 = **Gross margin 54.11 Cr (33.4%)**
- less CM1 costs (freight & forwarding 0.49 + loading 0.04 + commission & brokerage 4.71 + transportation 0.05 + discount allowed 0.90 = 6.19) = **CM1 47.92 Cr (29.6%)**
- less CM2 costs (advertisement 0.13 + selling/sales promotion 0.93 = 1.06) = **CM2 46.86 Cr (28.9%)**

Commission & brokerage (Rs 4.71 Cr) is treated as distributor/sales commission (CM1); it scales with sales and grows sharply in the projection years, consistent with a distributor/FICO commission line. Even if reclassified, CM2 stays strongly positive. Unit economics are clearly positive.

# Monedo Financial Services Private Limited - Structure Note

Entity: Monedo Financial Services Private Limited (CIN U65100MH2017PTC294521), Mumbai.
Type: RBI-registered NBFC-ND (non-deposit taking), digital lending licence received October 2018.
Origin: traces to Monedo GmbH (German fintech, formerly Kreditech); the Indian entity was acquired by
promoter Mr. Ashish Kohli (original share transfer from Monedo GmbH to Ashish Kohli in October 2020).

Structure: single regulated NBFC, own-book lending, plus a recently started co-lending arrangement.
- Own book: ~94% of total AUM.
- Co-lending (off book): with Kaleidofin Capital Private Limited, escrow/servicer model, started September 2025. Off-book AUM ~6% of total.
- Subsidiary: Monedo Corporate Services Private Limited (investment in subsidiary ₹20.09 Cr on the balance sheet). This entity also lends ₹21 Cr back to the NBFC as an inter-corporate deposit at 7%. Related-party circularity to be examined in diligence.
- Related travel-fintech: Itraveleo Private Limited (Mihuru), commission paid by the NBFC; this is the "travel financing" vertical referenced in the company brief.

Products / verticals (own book, Mar 2026):
- Supply Chain Financing (term loan + invoice financing): the bulk of AUM, ~93%. Anchor-network linked, short tenure, self-liquidating.
- Healthcare Financing: reimbursement of hospital expenses; small and declining share.
- SME / working-capital loans: small share.
- Other (incl. travel, vocational education): minor.

Ownership (fully diluted, 31 Mar 2026): Mr. Ashish Kohli ~91% (equity + CCPS), CCPS holders ~9%
(Nurture Ventures LLC and others). Promoter has been funding equity himself (₹12.93 Cr Aug 2025,
₹10 Cr Feb 2026).

Management: Ashish Kohli (Founder & CEO), Rakesh Ganti (CFO & Head Treasury), Karanjeet Gambhir (CRO),
Vipin Poddar (CBO), Ranjan Barua (CTO), Sumita Almeida (Head HR), Ashit Joshi (CISO). Total staff 44
(down from 100 at FY25 as the book consolidated into fewer, larger supply-chain exposures).

External rating: India Ratings (Ind-Ra) IND BBB-/Stable, assigned 15-17 October 2025, on ₹100 Cr bank
loan facilities.

Financials used: company financial model (Annexure 12, with FY22-FY25 audited actuals and FY26 actuals
to Mar 2026 from monthly MIS), audited FY24/FY25 (scanned), DNBS-4B regulatory return (Dec 2025), and
the operational annexures (portfolio cuts, PAR, collection efficiency, borrowing profile, top-20 borrowers,
shareholding, related-party, co-lending agreement). All figures sourced inline in the note.
